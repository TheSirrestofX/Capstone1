Boating

Enjoy a peaceful escape on Baguio's serene lakes with a relaxing boat ride. Glide through calm waters surrounded by lush greenery and cool mountain air, offering a perfect opportunity to unwind and take in the natural beauty of the area. Ideal for solo adventures or family outings.

Discover Serene Waters and Scenic Views

Burnham Park, nestled in the heart of Baguio City, offers a picturesque boating experience that invites you to explore its tranquil man-made lake. As you paddle along the water, take in the scenic beauty of lush gardens, tall pine trees, and vibrant flower beds that surround the park. The peaceful waters provide a calming backdrop, perfect for leisurely boat rides with friends or family.

Wildlife and Nature Encounters

While boating on Burnham Park's lake, you're likely to encounter local wildlife. Look for various bird species flying overhead or perched on the surrounding trees. The park’s serene atmosphere also provides a perfect opportunity to spot fish swimming just beneath the surface of the water.

Family-Friendly Boating Adventures

If you’re new to boating or looking for a relaxing family outing, Burnham Park offers calm waters ideal for beginners. Rowboats and pedal boats are available for rent, and the gentle pace ensures a safe and enjoyable experience for all ages. Life jackets are provided for safety, making it an ideal location for first-time boaters.

More Adventurous Options

For those looking for more of a challenge, consider rowing around the lake, exploring its various nooks and crannies. The park’s open space allows for a slightly more dynamic experience, especially if you take your time navigating the calm yet expansive water.

Prepare for Your Boating Experience

Before you set out, remember to wear comfortable clothing, and bring essentials like sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun. Be sure to stay hydrated, and consider bringing along snacks for a peaceful lakeside picnic once your boating adventure is complete.

Rental & Tour Information

Boat rentals are readily available around Burnham Park, and the prices are generally reasonable. Rentals include both rowboats and pedal boats. It's best to check availability during peak tourist seasons, as the park can get quite busy, especially during weekends and holidays.

Safety and Rules

Safety is a priority in Burnham Park, so all boaters are required to wear life jackets. It’s also recommended to check the weather conditions before heading out, as sudden changes in weather can occur in the area. If you're unfamiliar with the park, guided tours are available to help you navigate the lake and make the most of your visit.

Whether you're seeking a relaxing day on the water or an opportunity for a family adventure, boating at Burnham Park offers a wonderful way to connect with nature and enjoy the beauty of Baguio City.
