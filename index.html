THE CITY OF PINES: BAGUIO CITY

Nestled high in the Cordillera Mountains, Baguio City, the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," beckons travelers with its cool climate,
stunning natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage. This enchanting city offers a refreshing escape from the tropical heat
that invites you to immerse yourself in its serene atmosphere and explore its myriad attractions.
