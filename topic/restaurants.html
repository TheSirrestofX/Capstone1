Restaurants

Sunny Lake offers a range of accommodations, from charming cabins and family-friendly lodges to campgrounds under the stars. Relax and unwind in the perfect spot that fits your style and budget.

Agara Ramen

Slurp your way to ramen heaven at Agara Ramen, where authentic Japanese flavors meet Filipino comfort

Cocina Del Sol

Enjoy a casual dining experience at Cocina Del Sol, where hearty meals, craft beers, and a laid-back vibe make it a local favorite for lunch and dinner.

The Farmer's Daughter

Dine in style at The Farmer's Daughter, where creative dishes and an elegant atmosphere meet stunning lake and mountain views. Perfect for romantic dinners or special occasions.

Tajimaya Charcoal Grill

Savor fresh, locally inspired dishes with a stunning view of Sunny Lake. Tajimaya Charcoal Grill combines delicious cuisine with a relaxed lakeside ambiance perfect for any occasion.
