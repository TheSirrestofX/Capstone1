Wellness

Unwind with a peaceful nature walk, enjoy a relaxing spa day, or simply take in the calming beauty of Baguio’s lush landscapes. Baguio is your perfect retreat to relax, rejuvenate, and reconnect with nature.

Mindful nature walk

Find balance and tranquility with outdoor nature walks through Baguio’s serene landscapes. Perfect for all levels, these walks offer a unique way to connect with nature and explore the beauty of the mountains, forests, and cool, refreshing air.

Spa and relaxation

Indulge in ultimate relaxation in Baguio, where nature meets tranquility. Rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit with tailored treatments in serene mountain settings, surrounded by lush greenery and cool, refreshing air.

Nature Yoga

Find balance and tranquility with outdoor yoga sessions amidst the peaceful embrace of Baguio's forests. Perfect for all levels, these classes offer a unique way to connect with nature, as you practice yoga surrounded by towering trees, fresh mountain air, and the soothing sounds of the forest.
